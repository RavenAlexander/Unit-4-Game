Crystals Collector!

How to play:

  • There are four crystals displayed on the page.
  • You will be shown a randomly assigned number at the start of the game.
  • When you click on a crystal, it will add a specific amount of points to your total score. The value of the crystal is hidden until you click on it. Choose wisely!
  • You win if your total score matches the assigned number.
  • You lose if your score goes above the assigned number.
  • The value of the crystals changes at the start of each game to keep you guessing!
